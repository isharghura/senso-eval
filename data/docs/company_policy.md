# Company Policy Document

## Remote Work Policy
Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week. Office presence is required on Tuesdays and Thursdays.

## Leave Policy
- Annual leave: 20 days per year
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Public holidays: 12 per year

## Office Hours
Standard office hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, with 1 hour lunch break from 1 PM to 2 PM.

## Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professionalism and respect in all interactions. Zero tolerance for harassment.

## Expense Policy
Business expenses must be pre-approved. Submit receipts within 7 days for reimbursement.
